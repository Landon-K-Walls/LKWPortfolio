Why I keep going no matter what

I have always enjoyed the act of making things. The rush of finishing something difficult or challenging is one of the best feelings in the world for me. That chest-fluttering feeling of victory from overcoming a barrier or finding a right path always overpowers any hesitence or resistance to starting a task or project. That's why I always give 100% in anything I do. Because, no matter how many times I fall down, there's always a fist pumping victory on the other side.

How I got into development

I have always used and tinkered with computers, but the first time I wrote a line of code was in python about 12 years ago, right before my family and some friends took a camping trip to the Guntersville camping grounds in northern Alabama. I remember being so absorbed in it that the 4 day long trip was over in a flash and I hadn't talked to anybody. I'll admit that it was rude of me to isolate myself like that, but it did spark a small fire that would only grow bigger over the years.
